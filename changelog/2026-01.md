# January 2026

## January 31

### New

- **BAIT transaction history** — Anglers can now view their full BAIT earn-and-spend history from their profile.

### Improved

- **Submission review queue** — Captains can now bulk-approve or bulk-reject pending submissions with a single action.
- **Angler profile pages** — Profiles now show lifetime stats including total catches, species count, and derbies entered.

### Fixed

- **Leaderboard tie-breaking** — When two anglers have the same score, the earlier submission now correctly ranks higher.

---

## January 24

### New

- **Pro tier launch** — The Pro membership ($100/mo) is now available, giving sponsors and tournament organizers access to advanced analytics, branded derbies, and priority support.

### Improved

- **Derby creation wizard** — Captains can now duplicate an existing derby's settings when creating a new one.
- **Push notification preferences** — Anglers can choose which notifications they receive (submissions, leaderboard changes, derby reminders).

---

## January 17

### Improved

- **Hero shot guidelines** — Added on-screen framing guides during the hero shot step of BHRV to improve photo quality and reduce rejections.
- **Captain analytics** — Derby analytics now include a participation-over-time chart and species breakdown.

### Fixed

- **Invite links** — Fixed an issue where invite links expired prematurely for multi-week derbies.

---

## January 10

### New

- **Multi-species derbies** — Captains can now create derbies that track multiple target species with separate leaderboards per species.

### Improved

- **Release video compression** — Release videos are now compressed client-side before upload, reducing data usage by roughly 60%.

### Fixed

- **Password reset flow** — The password reset email now correctly links back to the app instead of a broken URL.
