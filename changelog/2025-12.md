# December 2025

## December 20

### New

- **Catch verification (BHRV)** — Launched the full Bump-Hero-Release-Verify workflow for tamper-proof catch submissions. Every catch now goes through bump board measurement, hero shot, release video, and blockchain verification.

### Improved

- **Onboarding flow** — New anglers now see a guided walkthrough on first login covering how to join a derby and submit a catch.

---

## December 13

### New

- **Captain Guide** — Published a comprehensive guide for tackle shop operators covering derby creation, prize setup, QR code signups, and analytics. Available at [docs.derby.fish/documentation](https://docs.derby.fish/documentation/).
- **Angler Guide** — Published a guide for anglers covering how to join derbies, submit catches, and earn BAIT. Also at [docs.derby.fish/documentation](https://docs.derby.fish/documentation/).

### Improved

- **Derby detail pages** — Added countdown timers, participant counts, and prize breakdowns to public derby pages.
