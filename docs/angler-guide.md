# Angler Guide

Welcome to DerbyFish. This guide covers everything you need to know about joining derbies and submitting catches.

## Joining a Derby

There are two ways to join:

1. **QR Code** — Scan the QR code at a participating tackle shop
2. **Direct Link** — Visit derby.fish and browse available derbies

After selecting a derby, pay the entry fee (if applicable) and you are in.

## Submitting a Catch

DerbyFish uses the BHRV verification system to ensure fair competition:

1. **Bump Board** — Place your fish on a measuring board and take a clear photo
2. **Hero Shot** — Take a photo of yourself holding the catch
3. **Release Video** — Record a short video of you releasing the fish
4. **Verify** — Submit through the app. AI verifies the species and blockchain timestamps the catch

## Leaderboards

Live leaderboards update as catches are verified. Scoring depends on the derby rules:
- Biggest single fish
- Total weight
- Most fish caught
- Species variety

## Prizes

When a derby ends, winners receive redemption codes via the app. Take your code to the hosting tackle shop to claim your prize.

## BAIT Currency

BAIT is the in-app currency used for entry fees and prize pools. You can earn BAIT through:
- Membership ($1/mo from your subscription goes to prize pools)
- Winning derbies
- Seasonal competitions
