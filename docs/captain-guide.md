# Captain Guide

Captains are tackle shop operators who host fishing derbies on DerbyFish. This guide walks you through setting up your shop and running your first derby.

## Getting Started

1. Sign up at derby.fish/for-shops or book a demo
2. Create your shop profile (name, address, logo)
3. Access your captain dashboard at captain.derby.fish

## Creating a Derby

From the captain dashboard:

1. Navigate to **Derbies > Create New Derby**
2. Fill in derby details (name, dates, species, rules)
3. Set entry fees and prize structure
4. Configure rankboard scoring (biggest fish, most fish, species variety)
5. Review and publish

## Setting Up Prizes

Prizes drive angler participation. You can offer:

- **Physical prizes** — rods, reels, tackle boxes
- **Gift cards** — store credit for your shop
- **Store credit** — redeemable at checkout
- **Promo codes** — percentage discounts

When a derby ends, winners receive redemption codes. They bring the code to your shop and you validate it through the captain dashboard.

## QR Codes for In-Store Signups

Every derby gets a unique QR code. Print it and display it at your counter. When anglers scan it, they land on the derby join page where they can sign up and pay the entry fee.

## Managing Submissions

Anglers submit catches through the app using the BHRV flow:
1. **Bump board** — photo of fish on measuring board
2. **Hero shot** — angler holding the catch
3. **Release video** — fish being released
4. **Verify** — blockchain timestamp and AI species verification

You can review submissions in the captain dashboard under **Fish > Submissions**.

## Seasons

Seasons let you run recurring competitions across multiple derbies. Anglers who join a season accumulate points across all included derbies.

## Analytics

The captain dashboard provides:
- Angler signup rates
- Revenue from entry fees
- Prize redemption tracking
- Derby engagement metrics
