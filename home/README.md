# DerbyFish

DerbyFish is a sportfishing competition platform that makes fishing derbies fair, fun, and cheat-proof.

## What is DerbyFish?

DerbyFish powers fishing competitions with blockchain-verified catches. Our BHRV system (Bump board, Hero shot, Release video, Verify on-chain) ensures every catch is legitimate.

## For Anglers

- Join local derbies hosted by your favorite tackle shops
- Compete on live leaderboards
- Win prizes redeemable at participating shops
- Build your fishing reputation with verified catches

## For Tackle Shops (Captains)

- Host branded fishing derbies that drive foot traffic
- Set up custom prizes, entry fees, and competition rules
- Get a branded captain dashboard
- QR codes for in-store signups — anglers scan and join instantly

## For Sponsors

- Sponsor individual derbies, prize pools, or entire seasons
- Get logo placement on leaderboards and derby pages
- Track engagement and ROI through the captain dashboard
