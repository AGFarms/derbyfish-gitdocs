# Troubleshooting

## Common Issues

### "Catch submission failed"

- Check your internet connection
- Ensure all four BHRV steps are complete (bump board, hero shot, release video, verify)
- Make sure photos are clear and well-lit
- Try resubmitting after a few minutes

### "Species not recognized"

- Use a clear photo with the fish against the measuring board
- Ensure good lighting (avoid harsh shadows)
- If the AI cannot identify the species, a manual review will be triggered

### "QR code not scanning"

- Ensure the QR code is printed clearly and not damaged
- Try adjusting your phone's camera distance
- Open the camera app directly (not through another app)

### "Cannot create derby"

- Verify you have a Captain or Pro account
- Check that your shop profile is complete (name, address required)
- Ensure the derby dates are in the future

### "Prize redemption code invalid"

- Codes are single-use and expire after the redemption window
- Check that the code matches the derby (codes are derby-specific)
- Contact the derby captain if the issue persists

### "Payment failed"

- Verify your payment method is up to date
- Check that your card has not expired
- Try a different payment method
- Contact support if the issue continues

## Contact Support

For issues not covered here, reach out at support@derby.fish.
