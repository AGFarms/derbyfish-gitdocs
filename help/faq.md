# FAQ

## General

### What is DerbyFish?
DerbyFish is a sportfishing competition platform. Tackle shops host branded fishing derbies, anglers compete on live leaderboards, and catches are verified using our BHRV system with blockchain timestamps.

### How much does it cost?
Members pay $2/month. Captains (derby hosts) pay $6/month. Pro shops pay $100/month for branded derbies and analytics.

### What is BAIT?
BAIT is the in-app currency used for entry fees and prize pools. A portion of every membership goes into the prize pool as BAIT.

## For Anglers

### How do I join a derby?
Scan the QR code at a participating tackle shop, or browse available derbies at derby.fish.

### How do I submit a catch?
Open the app and follow the BHRV flow: take a bump board photo, a hero shot, a release video, then submit for verification.

### How do I claim prizes?
Winners receive a redemption code in the app. Bring the code to the hosting tackle shop and they will validate it.

### What fish species are supported?
DerbyFish AI can identify most freshwater and saltwater game fish. Each derby specifies which species are eligible.

## For Captains

### How do I host a derby?
Sign up for a Captain or Pro account, create your shop profile, then use the derby creation wizard in the captain dashboard.

### Can I customize the prizes?
Yes. You can offer physical prizes, gift cards, store credit, or promo codes. Set them up in the Prizes section of the captain dashboard.

### How do QR codes work?
Each derby gets a unique QR code. Print it and display it in your shop. When anglers scan it, they land on your derby's join page.

### Can I run multiple derbies at once?
Yes. Captain and Pro accounts can run unlimited concurrent derbies.
